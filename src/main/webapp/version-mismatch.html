Your Arctic client version is to old

Why do I get this message?

The version of the code running in your browser doesn't match the version on the ArticWeb server. To avoid problems running the Arctic application please clear the browser cache.

How do I clear the browser cache?

That depends on your browser

  • Chrome

    • Ctrl+F5 (Windows/Linux)
    • Hold Shift and click the Reload button (Mac)
    Or
    1. Goto chrome://settings/content/cookies
    2. Find arcticweb.e-navigation.net and click it
    3. Delete all Applicationcache entries
    Or
    1. Hit F12 to open developer tools
    2. Click the Network tab
    3. Check the "Disable cache" check box and hit F5

  • Firefox

    • Hold down the Shift key and left-click the Reload button
    • Press "Ctrl + F5" or press "Ctrl + Shift + R" (Windows,Linux)
    • Press "Command + Shift + R" (Mac)
    Or
    1. Goto the Options menu
    2. Go to the Advanced tab and choose thw Network
    3. In the Offline web content choose the "https://arcticweb.e-navigation.net" entry and click remove
    Or
    1. Hit F12 to open developer tools
    2. Click the Network tab
    3. Check the "Disable cache" check box and hit F5

  • Other browsers and mobile devices

    Please search for "clear browser cache for specific site"
